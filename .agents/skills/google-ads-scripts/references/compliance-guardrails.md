# Ad Copy Compliance Guardrails

Evolance Wellness sells dietary supplements. All ad copy must pass FDA/FTC compliance before going live. This is Tier 0 — non-negotiable. Non-compliant copy has previously nearly gotten the ad account banned permanently.

**Rule: When in doubt, choose the more conservative language.**

---

## Absolute Prohibitions

Never use any of these in ad copy, landing pages, or any customer-facing content:

### Disease Claims
- "cures", "treats", "prevents", "diagnoses", "heals", "fixes"
- Named diseases: insomnia, depression, anxiety disorder, diabetes, ADHD, PTSD
- Disease-adjacent: "anti-inflammatory", "anti-anxiety", "anti-cancer"

### Medical Claims
- "I'm a doctor and I recommend..."
- "Doctors say...", "Healthcare providers suggest..."
- "Before I prescribe...", "Instead of [drug]"
- "Replaces your medication", "Better than prescription meds"

### Unsubstantiated Absolutes
- "Scientifically proven" (without specific citation)
- "Guaranteed results", "100% effective"
- "Medical breakthrough", "Miracle formula"

### False Urgency
- "Limited time" (without real deadline)
- "Only X left" (without actual inventory data)

### Google Ads Formatting Violations
- ALL CAPS in headlines or descriptions
- Repeated punctuation (!!!, ...)
- More than 1 exclamation mark per ad
- Phone numbers in ad text (use call extensions)

## Approved Language Patterns

These structure/function claims are FDA-compliant:

| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| "Supports healthy [function]" | "Supports healthy sleep patterns" |
| "Helps maintain [normal function]" | "Helps maintain calm focus" |
| "Promotes [natural process]" | "Promotes natural relaxation" |
| "May support [body system]" | "May support stress response" |
| "Helps your body's natural [process]" | "Helps your body's natural wind-down" |
| "Clinically studied for [function]" | "Clinically studied for sleep support" |
| "Formulated with [ingredient]" | "Formulated with Affron saffron" |
| "Contains [ingredient], studied for [X]" | "Contains L-Theanine, studied for calm" |

## Pre-Delivery Checklist

Before any ad goes live, verify:

- [ ] No disease claims (treat/cure/prevent/diagnose)
- [ ] No medical professional endorsements
- [ ] No prescription drug references
- [ ] No unsubstantiated absolute claims
- [ ] Only structure/function claims used
- [ ] Headlines are 30 characters or fewer
- [ ] Descriptions are 90 characters or fewer
- [ ] No ALL CAPS
- [ ] No more than 1 exclamation mark per ad
- [ ] Landing page URL is correct and live
- [ ] UTM parameters are properly formatted
